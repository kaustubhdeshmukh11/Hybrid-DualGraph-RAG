SECTION 1: LEAVE POLICY
-----------------------
1.1 Annual Leave: Employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per year.
1.2 Sick Leave: 12 days of sick leave per year. Medical certificate required for 3+ consecutive days.
1.3 Casual Leave: 6 days per year, cannot be carried forward to next year.
1.4 Maternity Leave: 26 weeks paid leave for female employees.
1.5 Paternity Leave: 2 weeks paid leave for male employees.
1.6 Bereavement Leave: 5 days for immediate family members.

SECTION 2: WORK FROM HOME POLICY
--------------------------------
2.1 Employees may work from home up to 3 days per week after completing 6 months.
2.2 WFH requests must be submitted 24 hours in advance via the NexPortal system.
2.3 Core hours (10 AM - 4 PM IST) must be observed even during remote work.
2.4 Internet allowance of Rs. 1,500/month provided for regular WFH employees.

SECTION 3: EXPENSE REIMBURSEMENT
--------------------------------
3.1 Travel: Economy class flights for domestic, business class for international (8+ hours).
3.2 Daily meal allowance: Rs. 800 for domestic travel, $50 for international travel.
3.3 Hotel booking limit: Rs. 5,000/night domestic, $200/night international.
3.4 All expenses must be submitted within 15 days of incurring via ExpenseBot app.
3.5 Receipts mandatory for claims above Rs. 500.

SECTION 4: PERFORMANCE REVIEW
-----------------------------
4.1 Reviews conducted bi-annually in April and October.
4.2 Rating scale: 1 (Needs Improvement) to 5 (Exceptional).
4.3 Employees rated 4+ are eligible for the "Star Performer Bonus" of 15% salary.
4.4 Two consecutive ratings below 2 may result in Performance Improvement Plan (PIP).

SECTION 5: EQUIPMENT POLICY
---------------------------
5.1 Laptops provided: MacBook Pro 14" for developers, Dell Latitude for others.
5.2 Monitor allowance: One 27" external monitor provided upon request.
5.3 Equipment must be returned within 7 days of last working day.
5.4 Damage due to negligence will result in 50% cost recovery from final settlement.

SECTION 6: CONFIDENTIALITY
--------------------------
6.1 All project code is property of Nexora Technologies.
6.2 NDA binding for 2 years post-employment.
6.3 Client information sharing strictly prohibited outside project teams.
6.4 Violation may result in immediate termination and legal action.
